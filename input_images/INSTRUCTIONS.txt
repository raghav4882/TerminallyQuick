Welcome to TerminallyQuick v2.0!

This is your default input folder.

HOW TO USE:
1. Drag and drop your images into this folder.
2. Run the TerminallyQuick launcher for your OS.
3. Follow the text prompts to optimize your images.

CUSTOM PATHS:
You can also process images from any other folder by choosing 
the [C] Change Path option at the start of the application.
When prompted for a path, you can drag any folder from your 
desktop into the terminal window.

SUPPORTED FORMATS:
PNG, JPEG, WEBP, AVIF, HEIC, CR3 (RAW), BMP, TIFF, ICO, PDF.
